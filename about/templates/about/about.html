Why the focus on Dublin?

Dublin has become an increasingly popular city for new parents, offering the perfect mix of economic opportunity, community spirit, and family-friendly amenities. While the modern world often feels isolated due to technology, Dublin provides a vibrant environment where parents can truly come together and thrive.

One of the key reasons behind this trend is Dublinâ€™s booming economy, particularly in the tech and finance sectors. With stable employment opportunities, many flock to "the big Smoke" from all over Ireland, but also from outside of Ireland. Meaning that people settle in Dublin, many of them do not have family nearby and friends that are made, often move away again after a couple of years.

This only increases the need for a platform that can provide support from one parent to another. That can connect parents from all layers of the community and that can provide the opportunity to create your own circle of 'chosen' family and friends that are there in a time of need.
